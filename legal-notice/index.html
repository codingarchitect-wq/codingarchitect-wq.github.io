Privacy Policy

This website respects your privacy and is committed to transparency about what data we collect and how we use it.

Analytics

We use the following analytics services to understand how visitors use this site:

  • Microsoft Clarity - Provides heatmaps and statistics to help improve user experience
  • PostHog - Provides product analytics to understand site usage patterns

Cookies

When you first visit this site, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can choose to:

  • Accept - Analytics cookies will be enabled, allowing us to collect usage data
  • Deny - Analytics will run in limited mode without persistent cookies

Your preference is stored in your browser’s local storage and remembered for future visits.

Data Collected

When analytics are enabled, the following data may be collected:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Browser type and device information
  • Approximate geographic location (country/region level)
  • Click patterns and scroll behavior

Data Not Collected

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (unless you voluntarily provide it via contact forms)
  • Payment or financial information
  • Precise location data

Your Rights
